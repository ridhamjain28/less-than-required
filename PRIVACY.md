# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 16, 2026

**Less Than Required** is a privacy-first browser extension designed to compress files locally on your device. We believe your data is yours, and we do not want it.

### 1. Data Handling
The extension locally handles files selected by the user and limited website content needed to detect upload controls and nearby file-size requirements. This may include the current page title, headings, and text surrounding a file input. This information is used only to provide the extension's file-compression functionality.

The extension does **not** transmit, retain, sell, or share files, website content, browsing activity, personally identifiable information, or usage analytics. The developer and third parties do not receive this data.

### 2. File Processing
All file compression (images and PDFs) happens **locally on your device** within your browser. No files are ever uploaded to any external servers, third-party services, or cloud providers for processing.

### 3. Website Access
To provide automatic compression at upload fields, the extension runs locally on HTTP and HTTPS pages. It inspects upload controls and nearby text for file-size requirements only. It does not retain or transmit page content or browsing activity.

All information handled by the extension is used solely for its disclosed purpose of helping users meet file-upload requirements. It is not used for advertising, profiling, creditworthiness, lending, or any unrelated purpose.

### 4. Third-Party Services
We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

### 5. Changes to this Policy
If we ever decide to change our privacy practices, we will update this document and release a new version of the extension clearly stating the changes. However, our core philosophy is that everything should happen locally.

### Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/ridhamjain28/less-than-required).
